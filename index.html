About Me

I am a GCHQ-accredited MSc Cyber Security graduate and Certified Ethical Hacker making a focused transition into offensive security. My career started in enterprise IT operations — configuring 250+ devices, running SOC-style incident investigations, and managing Active Directory across complex environments — which gave me an attacker's instinct for how systems break.

At Eurostop, I currently oversee end-to-end data integrity across enterprise EPOS platforms and third-party integrations, applying root-cause analysis and operational security thinking at scale. That same mindset — trace the data, find the anomaly, secure the boundary — is exactly what drives good penetration testing.

Outside of work, I actively sharpen my offensive skills on TryHackMe and HackTheBox, working through hands-on labs in enumeration, exploitation, and post-exploitation. My long-term goal is Red Teaming.
